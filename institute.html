About Readime Institute

Training the next generation
of researchers.

Readime Institute is our education and training arm. We run fellowships, workshops, and certification programs that teach the practical craft of rigorous, human-centered research.

From first-time researchers to seasoned professionals, our mentorship and coursework are built to be applied immediately — in the field, in the lab, and in the write-up.
